Questions Worth Answering

The short version.

Why no free trial?
The confidence is the pitch. A free trial tells you the product is unsure of its own value. We're not. $29 per month is less than one bad trade. If a single edge we surface doesn't pay for the year, we are not doing our job.
What does "quantified edge" actually mean?
Our AI calculates true_probability for a contract. The market shows you its implied probability (the price). Edge is the gap. If our analysis says YES is 68% likely and the market is pricing YES at 45%, that's a 23-point edge. You get the number, the direction, and the written reasoning behind it.
Is this legal?
We don't place bets for you. We don't give financial advice. We're an analysis tool. You make your own decisions on Kalshi or Polymarket. Both platforms are CFTC-regulated (Kalshi) or operate as information markets (Polymarket). Your jurisdiction and tax situation are yours to manage.
What's the accuracy rate?
We publish our accuracy dashboard to every First Mate and Ahab subscriber. Post-launch we show the real numbers, updated daily, with no cherry-picking. If the system isn't working, you'll see it in the data before we do. If it is, you'll see that too.
Bring Your Own AI?
First Mate and Ahab can plug in an OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter API key. Our picks, our prompts, your tokens. 300 queries per day. Zero cost to us, zero cost to you beyond what your provider charges. We built this because power users wanted it.
